About The Classic Cut

Established in 2010, The Classic Cut is more than just a barber shop; it's a community hub where gentlemen come to relax, converse, and leave looking their absolute best.

Our master barbers are trained in the latest techniques as well as the timeless traditions of the trade. We take pride in our attention to detail and our commitment to old-school customer service.

Step into our shop and step back in time to an era where quality and conversation were paramount.
